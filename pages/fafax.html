Fafax recipe

This is one of the most loved Traditional dishes in djibouti. it is a stew made with lamb meat, chillies and vegetables. A very good remedy for constipation and is a constant in every household.it is normally served with bread or rice as side dish.

    list of ingredients
  1. 1/4 lamb(for 4 servings)
  2. 1 onion
  3. 2 potatoes
  4. 1 carrot
  5. 2 green pepper
  6. 4 garlics

Instructions

start by chopping the onions and put it in a dish at medium fire with some oil

when the onion gets read add the potatoes and lamb , sprinkle some salt and let it cook for 15min

now add the rest of the vegetables carrot and green pepper plus the garlic

wait it to all mix and get the flavor.After 10min the stew will be ready to be enjoyed
