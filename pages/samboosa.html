samoosa recipe

Samboosa is a traditional snack and appetizer in east africa loved by a lot of people. It is also consumed in some part of west AFrica.

it has the shape of a pyramid and its made with dough filled inside with some minced meat and veggies. Although some people just prefer to have with only veggies.

Ingredients

  • half a pound of minced meat
  • 2 carrots
  • a mix of green veggies
  • dough

Steps

  1. spread the dough on a flat space and cut it in a triangle shape
  2. put the mix of minced meat and veggies in the center
  3. wrap it up in a triangle shape with using some wheat for sticking
  4. put it in a preheated oil. flip to the other side when its cooked
