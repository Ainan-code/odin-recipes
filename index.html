Djibouti is a country located in the horn of africa bordered by somalia and ethiopia. it is located on a strategic position on the entrance of bab el mandeb which is one of the most important trade route in africa.

The population of this small country is less than 1million and consists of three ethnic groups: Somali, Afar and Arab.
